Our Milestones & Achievements

Thursday, May 24, 2018

Vizag Seaport set a record by discharging 39,985 MT of Coking coal in a day at our Terminal from Panamax Vessel MV. JIA DA. This is the highest discharge rate achieved at our Terminal during the month of May 18. The Cargo importers are M/s SAIL .

Sunday, March 27, 2016

Vizag Seaport set a record by discharging 41,682 MT of Steam coalin a day at our Terminal from Panamax Vessel MV. MBA FUTURE. This is the highest discharge rate achieved at our Terminal during the month of March 16. The Cargo importers are M/s Swiss Singapore.

Friday, November 20, 2015

Vizag Seaport set a record by discharging 32,811 MT of Pet coke in a dayat our Terminal from Panamax Vessel MV. Key Action. This is the highest discharge rate achieved at our Terminal till date. The Cargo importers are M/s Acc & Acl Ltd.

Tuesday, October 27, 2015

Vizag Seaport set a record by discharging 32,576 MT of Pet coke in a dayat our Terminal from Panamax Vessel MV. Eternity Island. This is the highest discharge rate achieved at our Terminal during the month of October 15. The Cargo importers are M/s Prism cements.

Salient Features

  • Customs notified area with full pledged customs clearance facility inside the Terminal
  • Port Connectivity road adjoining the Terminal to facilitate easy transit of cargo by Road
  • Two Railway Sidings ( Terminal is an Independent Railway Wagon Booking Station with individual alphanumeric siding code)
  • One Road Weigh Bridge and 3 Nos. In-motion Rail Weigh Bridges
  • State-of-the-art, automatic and time controlled Dust Suppression System
  • Concrete block paved stockpile are with storage capacity up to 300,000 Tons
  • ISO 14001 Certified Terminal
  • TAMP Approved Tariff Structure
  • Zero or negligible Wastages / Spillages / Handling losses
